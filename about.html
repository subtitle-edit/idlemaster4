About IdleMaster

Revolutionizing Steam Trading Card Collection

Who We Are

IdleMaster is a cutting-edge application designed to automate the process of collecting Steam trading cards. Born from the need to simplify card collection for Steam users worldwide, our platform has evolved into the most trusted and efficient solution in the market.

We understand that manually idling games to collect trading cards can be time-consuming and tedious. That's why we created IdleMaster - to give you back your time while still allowing you to build an impressive Steam card collection and inventory.

Our Story

IdleMaster began as a simple project to solve a common problem faced by Steam users - the tedious process of collecting trading cards. What started as a basic automation tool has grown into a sophisticated platform used by tens of thousands of users worldwide.

Over the years, we've continuously improved our software based on user feedback, technological advancements, and changes in the Steam platform. Our commitment to excellence has made IdleMaster the go-to solution for Steam card collection automation.

Today, we're proud to be an open-source project with an active community of contributors and users who help shape the future of IdleMaster. We're grateful for the trust our users place in us and remain dedicated to providing the best possible experience.

Built with Passion

IdleMaster is developed and maintained by a dedicated team of developers and Steam enthusiasts who are passionate about creating quality software. Our open-source approach ensures transparency and allows the community to contribute to the project's success.
